Sell Your Toys

Ready to turn your collection into cash? Here is exactly how to get started with The Toy Outpost.

Step 1

Reach out

Call us at 443-424-8697 to book an appointment. You can schedule a time to bring your collection in, or set a time for us to come to your location and review it there.

Step 2

Send photos

Text photos of your items to 443-424-8697 or email photos to toyoutpostco@gmail.com. Pictures help us prepare and speed up the process.

Step 3

Get evaluated and paid

We pay cash. Final valuation depends on the toy line, subject matter, and condition of the items.

We also accept walk-ins, but our appraisers may not be on-site at all times, so appointments are the fastest route.

What we are primarily looking for

Toys from the 70's through today, including Star Wars, GI Joe, Masters of the Universe, Transformers, Silverhawks, Thundercats, Centurians, My Little Pony, Strawberry Shortcake, Lego, Funko, Barbie, Horror, and Sci-Fi.

Do not see your toys on this list? No problem. We would still love to see them, so reach out and tell us what you have.

Bring the legends. We will help you find the best next home for your collection.
